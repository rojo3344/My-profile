My Journey

I graduated with a degree in Business Administration, shortly after, I was fortunate enough to pursue and fulfill my dream of playing basketball professionally in Spain.

Uppon completion of my basketball career, my focus turned to update my knowledge to today's marketing environment. Therefore, I followed several online courses which led to the discovery of my new passion for coding and web development.
