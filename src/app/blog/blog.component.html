My Journey

My path to the tech world is not conventional, considering the fact that I am a self-taught developer and coming from a field not related to programming. I faced many obstacles during the transition, and it was due for the most part to people's views and expectations of an athlete. However, my dedication and perseverance turned coding into my new passion

In Coding, I found a safe haven to fill my desire to be challenged. I was fascinated to see so many similarities between programming and sports. You have to improve constantly to keep up with the environment because what works today is obsolete tomorrow.


How I Got Into Coding

Preceding the completion of my basketball career, my focus turned quickly to my academic studies. In order to be updated to today's marketing environment, I followed several online courses which eventually led to the discovery of my new passion for writing codes.

I was amazed to find out how much information is available online to the public free of charge. In fact, the majority of my programming skills do not come from the online classes, but from personal research. My only regret is that I wish I would have started to code earlier.
